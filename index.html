What we do

Four things we do, properly.

Performance is built, not bought. Test. Train. Track. Adapt. Every athlete that walks through the door gets a programme built around their data, their sport, and where they're at in their season — and we keep tightening it as the numbers move.

01 · Testing
Performance testing across speed, power, strength and core

5m and 10m sprints, broad jump, countermovement and Abalakov jump, drop jump RSI, isometric mid-thigh pull with bodyweight ratios, plank holds. Numbers in, numbers out — every test gets re-run on a cycle so progress is measured, not assumed.

How we test
02 · Strength & conditioning
Six fundamental movement patterns, leveled across the programme

Squat, lunge, hinge, push, pull, brace. Athletes work through progressions — back squat to bench, hex bar deadlift to tempo chin-up — at the level their movement and loading capacity allow. Earn the next level on the floor, not on the calendar.

Movement framework
03 · Sports science
Longitudinal data on every athlete, every season

Sports science isn't a buzzword here — it's how every programme gets written. We hold testing data across years so we can see what's actually changing, what's stalled, and what needs a different stimulus. The data drives the decisions.

Our methodology
04 · Programming
Built around the playing week, not the gym

If an athlete is playing competitively, the programme is built around match day. Strength focus when the calendar allows, MD+/-1 primers when it doesn't. S&C serves performance — it doesn't compete with it.

How we periodise
